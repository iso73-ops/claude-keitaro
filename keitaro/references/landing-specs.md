# Landing Page Specs by Vertical

## Universal Requirements

All landings must:
- Load in < 3 seconds
- Be mobile-responsive (60-80% of affiliate traffic is mobile)
- Have clear CTA above the fold
- Include tracking pixel/postback integration
- No broken images or missing assets
- SSL certificate active (https)

---

## Gambling / Casino

### Prelanding (Advertorial Style)
- Hook: success story, big win, new strategy
- Format: fake news article / blog post / review
- Elements: winner photo, payout screenshot, casino rating
- CTA: "Try your luck" / "Claim bonus" / "Start playing"
- Compliance: 18+ disclaimer, responsible gambling note

### Landing (Direct)
- Hero: bonus offer front and center ("Get 100% up to $500")
- Trust: license badges, payment method logos
- Urgency: timer, limited slots, "X people playing now"
- CTA: "Register" / "Get Bonus" / "Play Now"

### Key Elements
```
- Bonus value prominently displayed
- Registration form (email + password, max 2-3 fields)
- Casino brand logo
- Game thumbnails/previews
- Payment methods strip
- 18+ and responsible gambling disclaimer
```

## Crypto / Trading

### Prelanding (News/Success Story)
- Hook: "Local [city] man makes $X with new trading platform"
- Format: fake news article with financial expert quotes
- Elements: trading screenshots, profit charts, celebrity/expert photo
- CTA: "Start trading" / "Open free account"
- Compliance: risk disclaimer required

### Landing (Registration)
- Hero: platform interface preview, potential earnings
- Trust: regulation badges, media logos ("As seen on...")
- Form: name, email, phone (call center will follow up)
- CTA: "Register" / "Get Started" / "Open Account"

### Key Elements
```
- Trading platform preview/screenshot
- Minimum deposit amount clearly stated
- Profit calculator widget
- Testimonials with realistic returns
- Risk disclaimer (legally required in most GEOs)
- Phone field (critical for call center model)
```

## Nutra / Health & Beauty

### Prelanding (Advertorial)
- Hook: before/after transformation, doctor endorsement
- Format: blog article with product review
- Elements: before/after photos, ingredient breakdown, customer reviews
- CTA: "Order now" / "Get 50% off" / "Try risk-free"

```

- Doctor/expert endorsement
- Before/after gallery
- Ingredient list with benefits
- Bundle pricing table
- Order form (COD: name + phone + address)
```

## Dating

### Prelanding (Quiz/Matching)
- Hook: "Find your match" / "Singles in [city] looking for you"
- Format: simple quiz (3-5 questions) leading to registration
- Elements: attractive profile photos, match percentage
- CTA: "See matches" / "Start chatting"

### Landing (Registration)
- Hero: attractive profiles from user's area
- Form: gender, looking for, age, email
- Social proof: "X members online now"
- CTA: "Join free" / "View profiles"

### Key Elements
```
- Profile photos (age-appropriate for target demo)
- Location-based content ("Singles near [city]")
- Online counter ("X members online")
- Minimal registration (2-3 steps max)
- Age verification (18+)
- Privacy assurance text
```

## Finance / Loans / Insurance

### Prelanding (Calculator/Comparison)
- Hook: "Compare rates" / "See if you qualify" / "Calculate your savings"
- Format: comparison table, rate calculator, eligibility quiz
- Elements: bank/lender logos, rate comparison, savings amount
- CTA: "Check your rate" / "Get a quote" / "Apply now"

### Landing (Lead Form)
- Hero: savings amount / rate comparison
- Trust: financial institution logos, security badges
- Form: loan amount, purpose, credit score range, contact info
- CTA: "Get matched" / "See offers"

### Key Elements
```
- Loan/insurance calculator
- Rate comparison table
- Bank/lender partner logos
- SSL/security badges prominently
- Regulatory disclaimers (varies by GEO)

```

## Sweepstakes

### Prelanding (Win Notification)
- Hook: "Congratulations! You've been selected" / "Spin the wheel"
- Format: prize notification, wheel of fortune, gift selection
- Elements: prize images, urgency timer, confetti animation
- CTA: "Claim your prize" / "Enter now"

### Landing (Registration)
- Hero: prize image (iPhone, gift card, etc.)
- Form: SOI (email only), DOI (email + confirm), CC submit (card details)
- Urgency: timer, limited entries remaining
- CTA: "Enter" / "Claim"

### Key Elements
```
- Prize image (high quality, aspirational)
- Countdown timer
- "Only X prizes left" counter
- Simple form (fewer fields = higher CR)
- Terms & conditions link
- Winner testimonials
- Brand logo of prize (Apple, Samsung, etc.)
```

## E-commerce

### Landing (Product Page)
- Hero: product images (multiple angles, lifestyle)
- Price: original crossed out, sale price, savings %
- Trust: reviews, ratings, shipping info, return policy
- Urgency: stock counter, sale timer
- CTA: "Add to cart" / "Buy now"

### Key Elements
```
- High-quality product images (3-5)
- Price with discount clearly shown
- Star rating + review count
- Shipping info (free shipping threshold)
- Return/refund policy
- Size/variant selector
- Add-to-cart + buy-now buttons
- Related products
- Customer reviews section
```

## General Landing Page Structure

```
[Header: Logo + Navigation/Trust badges]
[Hero: Main offer + CTA]
[Social proof: Reviews/Testimonials/Media logos]
[Benefits: 3-4 key points with icons]
[How it works: 3 steps]
[More social proof: Detailed reviews]
[FAQ: 4-6 common questions]
[Final CTA: Repeat offer + button]
[Footer: Disclaimer + Legal links]
```

## Mobile Optimization Checklist
- [ ] CTA button is thumb-reachable (bottom 1/3 of screen)
- [ ] Form fields are large enough for mobile input
- [ ] No horizontal scrolling
- [ ] Images optimized (WebP, lazy loading)
- [ ] Font size >= 16px for body text
- [ ] Click-to-call enabled for phone numbers
- [ ] Sticky CTA bar on scroll
