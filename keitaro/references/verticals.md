# Vertical-Specific Benchmarks & Thresholds

Use these as defaults. User-specific data always overrides benchmarks.

## Gambling / Casino / Betting

| Metric | Kill | Warning | Good | Scale |
|--------|------|---------|------|-------|
| ROI | < -40% | -40% to -10% | -10% to 30% | > 30% |
| CR (reg) | < 5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | > 20% |
| CR (FTD) | < 0.5% | 0.5-1.5% | 1.5-4% | > 4% |
| EPC | < $0.10 | $0.10-0.40 | $0.40-1.00 | > $1.00 |
| Min clicks | 150 | — | — | 300 |

**Key metrics:** FTD (First Time Deposit), CPA per FTD, baseline deposit amount
**Typical payouts:** $50-200 CPA, 25-45% RevShare
**Top GEOs:** DE, AT, CH, CA, AU, NZ, NO, FI, BR, IN
**Traffic sources:** Facebook, Google, Push, SEO, Native, ASO
